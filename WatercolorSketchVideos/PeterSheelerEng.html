WATERCOLOR SKETCH VIDEOS

Drawing in watercolors using tutorials on Youtube

Peter Sheeler

Excellent author channel dedicated to watercolors and sketching. The artist paints nature - endless skies, spring birches, forest birds, and the human world - coastal lighthouses, village mills, ancient architecture. What do you find in common in its rural courtyards and city streets? The lessons are suitable for both beginners and experienced artists. The material is fed easily and consistently, the works are well reproduced.
