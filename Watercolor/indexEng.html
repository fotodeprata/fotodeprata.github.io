First steps. First drawings!

The site provides an overview of watercolor painting lessons on Youtube.

The authors of the video tutorials explain step by step how you can express essence and color using watercolors and how to achieve the desired result using special painting techniques.

Don't forget to like it!


What do you need to draw?


  • set of artistic watercolors,
  • soft round brushes of different sizes,
  • cellulose paper for drawing with watercolors, with a density of at least 200 g/m2,
  • paper tape to fix the sheet,
  • water,
  • palette,
  • liner or soft pencil.
